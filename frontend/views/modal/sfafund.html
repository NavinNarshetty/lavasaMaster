CHAMPIONSHIP FUND

TERMS AND CONDITIONS

The following is the eligibility criteria for the SFA Championship Fund, 2017:

  1. Only Registered Schools (schools that have paid the registration fee of Rs. 12,000/- to SFA Events Pvt. Ltd.) featuring in the top 10 schools of the SFA Championship Ranking Table shall be eligible for the prize money allocated under the SFA Championship Fund, 2017.

    For the sake of clarity:

    • Any school that has not registered with SFA and athletes from such schools are participating at the SFA Championship in their independent capacity shall not be eligible to receive any prize money;
    • A school which has been sponsored by SFA to play at the SFA Championship shall not be eligible to receive any prize money;
    • The prize money shall be awarded as per the ranking achieved on the SFA Championship ranking table and no registered school shall be moved up the table for the reason that a sponsored / unregistered school has featured in the top 10.
  2. The prize money shall be used towards:
    • Creation and/or betterment of sports infrastructure in the school.
    • Buying and/or renting quality equipment.
    • Awarding sports scholarship to students for training and development.
    • Hiring of experts in the field of sports.
    • Participation in district, state and/or national level tournaments.
    • Sports Apparel
    • Any other sports related expenditure to ensure quality sport.
    • The registration fee for SFA Mumbai, 2018.
  3. The prize money shall be paid by cheque issued in favor of the school and/or the trust/body managing the school and no cheques shall be issued any individual name.
