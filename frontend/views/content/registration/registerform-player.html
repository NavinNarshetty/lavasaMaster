Registration for SFA Championship 2018-2019 Mumbai is a 2-Step Process

STEP 1: Player Registration

Select a package, fill personal details and make payment.
(Receive confirmation email on verification of documents & successful payment. Proceed to STEP 2)

STEP 2: Sports Registration

Register for your sport as per your package

*In case you wish to participate in a team sport, kindly confirm your selection with your school authority.

We request you to keep the following details ready to ensure a smooth registration process:

  • Mobile Number which you wish to register with SFA (OTP will be sent)
  • Email ID which you wish to register with SFA (OTP will be sent)
  • Scanned copy of your Aadhar Card (with your full Date of Birth)/ Pan Card/ Birth Certificate
  • Scanned copy of your most recent School ID Card/ Report Card
  • Credit Card/ Debit Card/ Bank Details (in case of online payment)

* The applicant represents that the information provided is true and correct. SFA will keep confidential and protect your personal information except where disclosure is required or permitted by law.
