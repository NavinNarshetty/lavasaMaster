D.Y. Patil Sports Academy, Nerul.

DYPSA is dedicated to research, development and education in a variety of sports, with the aim of creating a centre of excellence that will put India on the global stage of competitive sport.

Dr.Vijay Patil, Founder and President of DY Patil Sports Academy believed that every athlete deserves quality training and playing facilities. And with the aim to create a sports academy to rival the best in the world, he built DYPSA that houses world class sporting facilities including a state-of-the-art stadium.
