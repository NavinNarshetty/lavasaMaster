FAQs

Schools

A) Which schools can register for SFA Mumbai 2017?

We welcome all schools from Mumbai and adjoining districts, including Navi Mumbai, Thane, Raigad & Palghar districts i.e. Aided, Unaided, Private and Government schools.


B) Should my school be from a certain board to participate in SFA Mumbai 2017?

There is no restriction on the school entries based on the education board. Schools from all boards: ICSE, CBSE, SSC, NIOS, IGCSE and IB are welcome to register for SFA Mumbai 2017.


C) Will both, co-ed and non-co-ed schools, be able to register?

Yes. All schools, co-ed and non-co-ed, are welcome to participate in SFA Championships. Athletes from any school registered with SFA can participate in SFA’s Annual Olympic style Inter-School Championship, SFA MUMBAI 2017.


D) HOW WILL PARTICIPATING IN SFA MUMBAI 2017 HELP MY SCHOOL?

SFA through its championships provides the biggest platform, for a school and its athletes to showcase their sporting talent across 25+ disciplines of sport. In order to encourage school athletes to continue pursuing their sport, SFA championships are designed to give all the school’s athletes the best sporting experience, by providing them facilities on par with those available at the international level, including : 

  • World Class Multi-Sporting Infrastructure and Facilities
  • High Quality of Governance & Competition (Certified officials, partnership with recognized associations, Attracting City’s best talent)
  • Access to On court Physio Care and hydration & nutrition centers
  • Talent scouting
  • School Ranking System

SFA creates online profiles for all the schools & it’s athletes. These profiles contain their sporting records along with all their match videos. These match videos help schools in showcasing their sporting talent to the world, providing further development to its current athletes and in encouraging more sporting participation in the school. Through the profiling system, SFA maintains the sporting records and progress of a school for life.

All registered schools get a school report card which documents their overall performance at the championships. This report card also helps in highlighting the school’s standing in sports among all school’s in the city. 


E) Do schools that have registered for previous SFA championships need to register again for SFA Mumbai 2017?

Yes, registration for any SFA championship is an annual affair and is valid only till the end of the respective year’s championship. Schools that wish to register must follow the registration process.


F) What is the registration fee for Schools to participate in SFA Mumbai 2017?

The registration fee for SFA Mumbai 2017 is Rs. 12,000 (Inclusive of all applicable taxes). This will allow a school to send any number of athletes (SFA Registered Athletes Only) across all the sports at SFA Mumbai 2017 as well as avail all services mentioned in point d.


G) Are athletes from a registered school expected to pay a separate registration fee to participate in SFA Mumbai 2017?

Yes, in the interest of maintaining a high quality of competition across all sports at SFA Mumbai 2017 a nominal fee of Rs. 200 will be charged to students from registered schools which will enable them to participate in all sports and avail all services as mentioned in point d Athletes from unregistered schools will be charged a registration fee of Rs. 600.


H) Will SFA provide transport to athletes from registered schools?

No, there will be no transport facilities that will be provided to athletes for SFA Mumbai 2017. Schools are expected to make their own transport arrangements to all the venues of SFA Mumbai 2017.


I) Is it necessary for schools participating in other sporting tournaments, to take an NOC from the governing bodies of those tournaments?

It is not necessary for schools to take an NOC from governing bodies of any other sports tournaments for e.g. MSSA, NMSA, RFYS etc. that they may be participating in, to register and participate for SFA Mumbai 2017.

Kindly note the SFA Championships will happen only for 15-20 days at a stretch across multiple sports. It is an Olympic style inter-school competition. We try and ensure no clash with any other interschool tournaments. We encourage schools and athletes to play as many tournaments all-round the year, to improve and excel in sports.


J) What is the SFA School Ranking System?

The SFA School Ranking System has been devised to showcase the ranking of all schools in the Mumbai City, Mumbai Suburban, Thane, Palghar and Raigad Districts basis their performance across multiple sporting disciplines at SFA Championship. Each medal won by an athlete/team of a school’s contingent in any event category, adds to the points tally of the school ranking system in the following manner:

  • i. Gold Medal = 5 Points
  • ii. Silver Medal = 3 Points
  • iii. Bronze Medal = 1 Point

K) How many Athletes can participate from the registered schools?

There is no restriction on the number of athletes that can participate across all 25+ sporting disciplines at SFA MUMBAI 2017.


L) How many sports can the registered school participate in?

Schools registered with SFA can participate in all 25+ sports that will be conducted at SFA MUMBAI 2017.


M) What if the school does not have athletes to participate in all the 25+ sports conducted at SFA MUMBAI 2017?

SFA through its championship encourages schools to identify the sporting talent that exits within their premise, across multiple sports. While it is not necessary to participate in all 25 disciplines of sport, the school should endeavor to give all its athletes an opportunity to represent their institution with pride.


N) How will I get my school’s match videos?

All the match videos will be uploaded and available through your athlete’s profiles on www.sfanow.in

E) Can an athlete/team participate in multiple age groups and events?

Each sport has a different set of participation guidelines. Kindly note the below mentioned guidelines are subject to SFA’s eligibility criteria & age cut off dates:

  • i. Team Sports (Basketball, Handball, Volleyball, Football, Hockey, Kabaddi, Kho Kho, Throwball, Water Polo)
    • 1. Athletes can register for multiple age groups but cannot participate in two teams of the same age group.
    • 2. A school can register unlimited number of teams in each age/event category EXCEPT in the case of FOOTBALL.
    • 3. In Football a school can register only ONE team per event category (For e.g. Boys U-16)
  • ii. Racquet Sports: (Badminton, Tennis, Table Tennis & Squash)
    • 1. An athlete can register in only one age group (U-11, U-13, U-15 & U-17) per event (Singles & Doubles). For e.g. In badminton if an athlete has participated in the U-11 Girls Singles category she is NOT ALLOWED to participate in U-13 Girls Singles, U-15 Girls Singles & U-17 Girls Singles categories. However, if she wishes to participate in a separate event i.e. Girls Doubles she may if she meets the cut-off dates.
  • iii. Combat Sports: (Karate, Taekwondo, Judo, Sport MMA, Boxing & Fencing)
    • 1. An athlete can register in only one age group (U-7, U-9, U-11 etc.) per event (for e.g. Kumite & Kata)
    • 2. An athlete can participate in only ONE weight category.
    • 3. Weight Categories do not apply to Fencing and Karate (Kata)
  • iv. Athletics:
    • 1. Individual Events:
      • a. An athlete can register in only one age group (U-6, U-8, U-10 etc.) per event (for e.g. 100m, Long Jump, Shot Put)
      • b. An athlete can register for a MAXIMUM OF 2 Individual events
    • 2. Relay:
      • a. Athletes can participate in multiple age groups if they meet the above-mentioned age criteria / cut-off dates.
      • b. A school can register only ONE team per event category.
  • v. Target Sports:
    • 1. Archery:
      • a. An athlete is expected to adhere to the following rules when it comes to selecting events for this sport:
        • i. U-10 - Indian Bow Only
        • ii. U-14 - Indian Bow AND/OR Recurve Bow OR Compound Bow
        • iii. U-17 - Indian Bow AND/OR Recurve Bow OR Compound Bow
    • 2. Shooting:
      • a. An athlete can participate in multiple event categories
  • vi. Swimming:
    • 1. Individual Events:
      • a. An athlete can register in only one age group (U-6, U-8, U-10 etc.)
      • b. An athlete can register for unlimited events Individual events pertaining to his/her age-group.
    • 2. Relay:
      • a. Athletes can participate in multiple age groups if they meet the above-mentioned age criteria / cut-off dates.
      • b. A school can register unlimited number of teams in each age/event category
  • vii. Board Games:
    • 1. Carrom:
      • a. An athlete can participate in ONE event category only
    • 2. Chess:
      • a. An athlete can participate in ONE event category only

F) What are the eligibility criteria to participate in SFA?

SFA, along with its sports partners have devised an eligibility criteria for each sport basis which athlete entries will be selected. These criteria have been devised so that the best athletes get a chance to represent their school, hereby making SFA an aspirational platform for athletes to compete.


G) Can a member of a team be replaced after registration?

Yes, any player of a team can be replaced one day prior to the commencement of SFA Mumbai 2017 by sending a request to info@sfanow.in via the registered email address


H) Will SFA will provide sporting gear to athletes?

No, SFA will not be providing personal gear to any athletes All athletes are expected to bring their own sporting gear.

I) When will the draws and schedules will be announced for SFA Mumbai 2017?

All draws and schedules will be published on our website www.sfanow.in by the 30th of October 2017. All schools and athletes will be notified via emails and text messages on their registered email IDs and mobile numbers.


J) Will athletes/teams be allowed to appeal against the decisions of the match officials?

No, all officials at SFA Mumbai 2017 are certified officials and their decisions will be deemed final and binding. Any misconduct by an athlete or a team will result in disciplinary action from the Tournament Organising Committee.


K) Are athletes/teams allowed to make an appeal to check the age of an athlete?

No, there is no provision for an athlete / team to make an appeal to check the age of an athlete since the registration process will ensure no over age athlete participates in any sport. However, in case an official wish to verify the age of an athlete he/she is authorised to do so.


L) What procedure will be followed when an athlete reports to the venue’s main registration desk?

Athletes/Teams must report at the venue’s main registration desk 45 minutes prior to their schedule match time. On reporting each athlete will have to show their school ID and birth certificate to validate their details, after which their IDs will be verified and allocated. After this, athletes will have to proceed and report to the registration desk of the respective sports he/she will be participating in 15 minutes prior to their schedule match timing.


M) Is it mandatory for the school’s entire contingent to arrive at the venue at the same time, even though different athletes have matches on different days or different times?

No, it is not necessary for the school’s entire contingent to arrive on the first day of the event. Athletes are allowed to arrive as and when they have their matches. We encourage schools to send in as many students and parents to support their athletes.


N) For doubles / team sports should all the members of the team be from the same school?

Yes it is mandatory that every member of a doubles team/ team sport should be a part of the same school.


O) Is it okay if every member of a doubles / team register separately or is it necessary for them to register at the same time?

It is not mandatory for all the members of a doubles / team to register at the same time, they can register separately.

B) What are the security arrangements made at the venue?

We recognize the fact that there will be close to 50,000 athletes and visitors across all the venues, and thus adequate security measures have been put in place to ensure that children have a safe and sport conducive environment.


C) Will visitors be asked for an ID proof at the venue?

Yes, visitors will be thoroughly checked and will be asked to produce an ID card to enter the venue. SFA Events Pvt. Ltd. holds all the rights to allow or disallow any individual into the venue at its sole discretion, in the interest of safety.


D) How do I arrive at the venue?

Schools and athletes are expected to make their own transportation arrangements to and from the venue. Driving directions will be displayed on the website. Private vehicles will not be allowed inside the venue premises. School buses will be allowed only till the designated drop-off point at the venue. School buses and vehicles will not be permitted to park inside the premises.


E) Are the athletes being provided with sports kits?

No, participants must carry their own sporting kits.


F) Is it compulsory for school officials to accompany athletes during the tournament?

It is not compulsory for school athletes to be accompanied by a school coordinator/manager during the event. However, parents, well-wishers and school coordinators and non-participating students are most welcome to encourage, cheer and support the athletes. There will be adequate safety and security measures at the venue.



G) Will the SFA Championship take place every year?

Yes, SFA is an Olympic style inter-school annual Championship. SFA aspires to be the most prestigious annual interschool sports competition, one that athletes and schools can look forward to at a specific time every year.


H) If an athlete is out of the competition, can he/she still come to the venue for the remaining days?

Yes, in case of an athlete not progressing to the higher stages of the tournament he/she is most welcome to be at the venue and cheer its school’s contingent. Also, he/she will have the opportunity to engage in other educational and recreational activities throughout the duration of the event.


I) What is the duration of the event?

The duration of SFA Mumbai 2017 is 23 days. The event will be conducted from the 1st to 23rd December. The schedule will be published on our website i.e. www.sfanow.in. The matches will be conducted from 8 am to 5.30 pm on all days of the event; However, schedules for different sports may vary. There will be special considerations for few sports which may need more time to be completed. Athletes and schools will be informed of the same latest by the 30th of October 2017.


J) What documents need to be carried by athletes during the tournament?

Every athlete must carry his/her school ID card along with his/her age proof at all times during the tournament. No athlete will be allowed to play the match if they do not have a valid age proof along with their school ID. Athletes must produce their ID cards as and when required during the tournament. These details are also mentioned on the website as well as in the sports registration rules and regulations.


K) Why do athletes need to report 45 minutes prior to their match time?

Every match at SFA is going to be captured on camera, thus it is crucial that every game starts at the schedule time. It is essential for the athletes to report at the venue at least 45 minutes prior to the game. There will be two registration desks – the main registration desk to report the entry of the athlete at the venue and the other will be a sports specific registration desk, where the athlete will have to report at the time his/her match is announced. Details regarding the reporting time for athletes are mentioned on the website as well as in the sports registration rules and regulations.
